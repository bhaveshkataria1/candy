About Us

With more than 130 years of chocolatier experience, Lindt offers authentic Swiss chocolate indulgences that elevate any occasion.

All Lindt chocolates indulgences are 100% produced in Kilchberg with sustainably sourced and carefully selected quality ingredients. Incomparable taste and excellent quality is the core focus for all our chocolate creations, promising our consumers the very best value for money offer they can find.
